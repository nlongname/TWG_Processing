DISCLAIMER: I have no idea what's going to happen!

LOST - live together, die alone

You were on Oceanic flight 815 until it crashed on the island. Miraculously, you are alive and relatively unscathed. However, if you get mortally wounded out here, you will most likely die.

You spent the first day getting your shit together and figuring out who all is alive:

1. reaper
2. dr. agon
3. guy
4. liquidangel
5. haplo
6. iron
7. that_kid
8. capt_dan
9. ben
10. schlingy
11. staticworth
12. dmgunner

rules

misc rules: don't try to cheat/break the game. it's not that robust. the balance is illusory at best. you should have fun whether you win or not.

here's how this works:
during day you can make posts and discuss things. movement occurs at the beginning of day.
during night you can do shit and plan where you are going next. action occurs at the beginning of night.

sometime during the day, PM me what you want to do during night
sometime during the night, PM me where you want to go during day

your movement gets processed at the beginning of day, i.e. you say during night you want to go to a certain square, and you will be there at the beginning of the day. at the beginning of day, you will be PMed the names of the other people in your square as well as any other pertinent information. then you can discuss things, plot, etc. PM me sometime before day end with what you'd like to do during night.

actions happen at the beginning of night.

you can post and communicate regardless of your location.

movement: tell me what square you would like to move to or toward. you can move through one undiscovered square per day. you can move through two discovered squares per day. cannot move diagonally between squares. the updated map will be posted at the beginning of every day.
if you keep trying to move into the ocean, i might get pissed, and you'll drown.

PM usage is unrestricted. please don't abuse this or go around copying PMs. stay in role or something, please! don't say "i'm jack," as names mean nothing anymore. it's like you get a fresh start on this island - your past means nothing now. if someone does this, report it to me (you'll get karma, they'll lose karma). go go honor system?

karma = game points. measure how well you played your role.
  
Actions:
scavenge wreckage (at crash site)
hunt for food
fish
gather fruits
attack people
trade items
at special squares, you can do stuff (you will find these)

lynching: there is no lynching. you simply gang up on them and try to kill them. if you're a total badass, you can do it yourself.  

combat: each player has a base strength of 1. you can only attack a player if they are in the same square as you. the side with the higher strength wins. the loser gets captured, and remains in that square until rescued, unable to talk or anything else. it's basically like being dead, except you can come back to life if rescued. if you tie, everyone involved gets injured.

information of interest

map

locations
Fuselage - You crashed here. You can scavenge wreckage, gather fruit, or go fishing.
Caves - houses a fresh-water spring, providing a near-unlimited source of fresh water. (water resource no longer needed)
Four-toed statue - An interesting landmark. Who built it? What does it mean?
