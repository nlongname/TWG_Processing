No, both are true.

In the first case, people would share information in other threads  because it will improve their chances of winning in that thread.
Example: Lynch Archie A. Archie B says "Yeah I was a wolf in that game, you got me." I treat this honesty as evidence that Archie B is human.

In the second case, people share information pre-death in a game, because it will improve their chances of winning in that thread - they can argue to stay alive and continue to contribute their votes and their count and any powers they have, they can convey valuable info to their team if human, and they will try to stay alive for sure if wolf and we'll get to hear more from them.
Example: Bandwagon on Archie A. Archie A claims portaler to save his life. Now we know he's either a wolf or a portaler (or Deathbutt).




So both are valid strategies and are not even mutually exclusive.
