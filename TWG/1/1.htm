Basic Rules:
There are 2 teams, wolves and humans.  The object for either team is to eliminate the opposing team. Rounds are divided into nights and days.  During the day, all players deliberate on who they think are wolves and cast votes to lynch someone.  During the night, wolves decide on a target and are allowed to kill someone off. The humans win when all the wolves are dead, and the wolves win when Wolves >= Humans.


Other Rules Include:
-No talking at night (This means no posting in this thread until I say the day has started)
-No private messaging to another player, meaning all discussion must happen publicly via posting. (Wolves are allowed to communicate to each other privately)
-You must post at least once per 'day', or you will automatically get a vote for yourself
-You may only vote once per 'day', meaning votes are permanent and cannot be changed once cast.
-The day will automatically end if one player receives a majority of votes
-Wolves and special roles must send their nighttime kills/guards/sights within a reasonable period of time, preferrably less than 24 hours
-You may not copy/post any private messages from myself or other players into the game
-No posting if you are dead (this should be obvious)
-No double posting
-All rules will be subject to my discretion

If you do not know if you are allowed to do something, ask first!
Violaters of any of these rules will be punished!


The Teams:
[The specific roles for this game have been hidden.]

Guaranteed Special Roles:
Seer - During the night, a seer has the ability to view one player to see their exact role (ie human guardian, or wolf, etc)

In general (as a human seer) you want to target people who you think may be a wolf.  If you find some fellow humans, you can try to form some kind of alliance.  Be careful, because if you tell others you are the seer, you will become a big target for the wolves.

Guardian - During the night, a guardian may choose one player to guard.  This player is protected from the wolves' kill that night. If a player is successfully guarded (meaning it blocks a night kill), I will announce who was guarded on the following day.  Guardian cannot guard themselves, and cannot guard the same person twice in a row.

In general you want to protect who you believe the wolves are going to kill.  Keep this in mind if the seer comes out.  Also, you can rarely be positive that anyone claiming to be a special is or not.
