TWG XLVII - There Are Fungus Among Us

Quote
It was just after Easter, and under the ice
Not a creature was stirring, except for the mycelium.

You are a happy colony of Ascomycota funguses of various types. But scum have invaded that peaceful substrate of Nirvana, the scummiest scum of all: algae.
These powerful algae can invade the body of any Ascomycete, gaining access to their memories, controlling their motion, infiltrating your society and disturbing your way of hyphae. This symbiotic relationship between algae and fungi is called a “lichen.”

The Yeerks algaes are here. Everywhere. If you think you don't know someone who is a Controller lichen, you're probably wrong. Your teacher, your friend, your sister, your mother and father. Any of them. All of them. And you'll never know. Until it's too late

Once per Day you can use your enzymes to break down another organism if you work together. The person with the most votes will be killed. You’ll have to do this each Day until all the lichens are destroyed.
But beware: during those days, the photosynthetic algae are gathering power. Each Night they will unleash that power, destroying one of the innocent funguses.

Days will be about 48 hours and will auto-extend if there’s still a lot of good discussion, in the GM’s opinion. They cannot be extended beyond about 72 hours unless everyone super wants them to. Hopefully we can take care of nights in 24 hours.

There is no mark and no sorcerer – the number of wolves will remain constant unless one is killed.
All roles will be familiar to most of you, something you could expect to find in previous TWGs. Wolf powers are possible; I decided that before attempting to come up with roles/balance the game.

Fungi win if all lichens are destroyed. Lichens win if they outnumber the fungi. No other win conditions.

Feel free to use Wikipedia.

Unlimited vote changes, lynch someone every day.
Don’t quote PMs, don’t talk at night, don’t post if you’re dead.
Everyone will get a PM.
