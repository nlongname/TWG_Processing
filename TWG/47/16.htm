But first, some speculation on the mystery role. First off, due to what Iron's said about win conditions, I don't think it's possible there is a third party.

Here's a list of common and not-so-common roles from MafiaScum: http://wiki.mafiascum.net/index.php?title=Roles

The names are usually different from what we use here, but here are some possibilities from those lists:

roleblocker - human or wolf, blocks role actions, possibly night kill.
vigilante - human, usually single-use NK power.
tracker - human, not common in mysticboards, chooses a person, sees who they targeted.
watcher - tracker in reverse. See if someone was targeted.
role cop - human. Role-seer, like the wolf-hero in this game.
vengeful - usually human, gets a kill if they are killed.
bomb - usually human, kills their killer.
bulletproof - human, immune to NK.

Anyway there are more possibilities, hopefully this keeps people from assuming it's a roleblocker or something else. The extra slot could also be vanilla human, vanilla wolf, or a wolf with another power.
