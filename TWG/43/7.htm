The day stretches on with deliberations, long 'bathroom breaks', and some halfhearted searching for clues. As the sun goes down and twilight sets in the small community gathers in the town square, where the Sheriff addresses the good people of Mystic River from atop a newly-erected gallows.

"My friends," he begins, "we are beset by a depraved and vicious enemy who lurks within our ranks. We have forever lost one of our most beloved friends, and Xazarus. I fear that tonight they will strike again if we do not act.

"This must be a community decision. I know it is not easy to send a neighbor to his demise but unfortunately there is no other way. We must find and destroy this madman, or madmen, before it is too late. Now. Let us come to a decision."

The crowd begins to argue amongst themselves. Accusations fly left and right until eventually Iron and Dr Agon are pulled apart from an imminent fistfight by their respective supporters.

"STOP!" Cries an unbiased third-party spectator. The commotion comes to a standstill at this unexpected interruption. "This is insane! Nobody's got any proof of anything whatsoever! We can't just randomly choose someone to die! We'd just be killing more innocents! Stop this madness!"

After a beat the crowd continues its commotion. The community is clearly divided between Iron and Dr Agon, neither of whom seem particularly upset by the threat of their impending doom.

The clocktower slowly rings its large bell nine times, the ominous ringing lays bare the tension between the quarreling townsfolk and the square falls silent. Who will make the first move?

Iron steps towards Dr Agon.

"Oh, I just realized, I've got more people on my side than you! Let's get him, guys!"

Dr Agon sighs and looks up at the gallows. "Well, so much for that."

The clock tower rings its large bell eight times.


Dr Agon is dead. Night 1 begins.
