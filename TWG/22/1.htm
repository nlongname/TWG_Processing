You are Cowboys, roughneck westerners who call no man "mister".  However, your camp has come into some trouble.  last night, your fellow roughhouser and good friend Dr. Agon was found dead.  and not only dead, but skinned too.  He was just lying out there in the middle of the camp, right by the campfire.  Who would do such a thing?  You all know that it must be Injuns.  and you come to the conclusion that some of them other cowboys in the camp must be Injuns in disguise!  So each night you will kill one of these "cowboys" until the deaths stop.

Rules:
1. I liked Guys 3 limit on vote changes so we shall use that.

2. we will try to do 48 hour days and 24 hour nights.  If I do not hear from you by the end of the time period (at night) I will roll a die to determine the target or the use of your powers or NK. (EXCEPTION - I will not end a day phase on a saturday or sunday, partly because I might not be online, and partly because I know a lot of you may not be either.  If the period deems it to end on a saturday or sunday I will extend the day to end on that monday.)

3. NO QUOTING THE PMS I SENT.  write in your own words please.

4. Tied lynches will have a die roll to determine the lynch.

5. THE DAY ENDS AT THE TIME THAT I SAY IT DOES NOT WHEN I POST TO END THE DAY.  you may post after then, but votes will not be counted.

6. No posting at night.

7. I reserve the right to add or change any rules as I see fit.

PMs are out so Day 1 start.  Day phase will end on Monday at 8PM
