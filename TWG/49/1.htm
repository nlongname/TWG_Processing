TWG XLIX - SECRETS. OF. THE UNIVERSE

In the beginning there was nothing... which exploded.

The time is just over 10-34 seconds after the Big Bang. The universe, though small, is expanding rapidly and is filled with chaos. This is the time when it shall be determined who shall dominate the universe: the positive Baryons, or the negative Antibaryons. Following this, insignificant societies of sentient species will wonder about why the winner won, but today (in the span of about 100 seconds) we shall learn the secret of Baryogenesis.

Approximately no time at all ago, you all awoke to find yourselves in a nearly empty universe. Approximately no time later, you discovered that in the blink of a Planck second, all of the sfermions in the universe had been annihilated, including every sup squark and sdown squark, every stau and smuon. Even the tiniest selectron sneutrino was destroyed.

The string theorists aren't wrong about the existence of strings, they're just 14 billion years out of date.

But only one thing could have destroyed the delightful sfermions: antimatter. Lurking in the dark matter is a group of particles called Antibaryons, seeking to destroy all you baryons. Only by working together can the baryons annihilate the antibaryons without getting destroyed themselves. Each night (or every few Planck seconds), you must choose one among you to annihilate, until you have either destroyed all of the antibaryons, or you have destroyed yourselves.

Good luck.

Days will always run from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM two days later. Nights will last from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM the following day, so days will be around 57 hours and nights around 33.

All roles are typical vanilla roles. Do not quote any PMs, and be careful about sharing any of your role flavor - it could potentially be used to identify your role.

There is no mark and no sorcerer.

Baryons win if all Antibaryons are destroyed. Antibaryons win if they outnumber baryons, or if only one baryon and one antibaryon remain.

There is no no lynch option.

Do not talk at night.

Do not quote PMs.

Ties will be resolved randomly.
