TWG XXVIII Dinner Party (It's done! Wolves win!)
« Topic Start: January 06, 2012, 04:11:58 PM »
  • Quote
After receiving a mysterious letter from a Mr. Cyan you each set out to the giant mansion located on the edge of town.
The mansion has always looked a little run down and out of place, but the promise of grand riches and treasures pulls you forward.
As you enter you find the inside to be well maintained and very comfortable.
Everyone gathers around a large dinner table, located close to the main entry room, and anxiously await their host to join them.

A bolt of lightning causes the lights to dim and forces everyone to focus towards the windows.
In the mild confusion no one notices a masked dark figure stepping into the room and taking a seat.
He raises his fork to his glass and begins talking in a voice that disrupts the silence that had settled in the room.


Gentlemen I regret to inform you that Mr. Cyan was a ruse and that such a person never existed.

Wait, wait. Please stay seated. Although Mr. Cyan is fake I can assure you that the fortune is very real, and very large.

Gentlemen some of you will be leaving this mansion a great deal richer, however some of you won't be leaving at all.

No no, do not try to leave. You will find that all the doors and windows are sufficiently barred and locked.

Among all of you are a few friends of mine that for some reason or another want you dead. You probably don't know them, but they hold quite a grudge on you for whatever reason.

The doors will be unsealed when the blood thirsty grudge holders outnumber the upstanding citizens, or when the citizens murder all the grudge bearers.

We will be keeping this soiree civil with a few basic rules for common curtsy.

Murderers will only be given the chance to kill someone at night and may only kill once per night.

Citizens may vote to lynch one person that they think is a murderer per day. There are no limits to votes, but please remember to vote

Rules are pretty much the same as all other games. No talking at night or if you are dead. Kill all the wolfs (murderers) or out number the humans (citizens.) Don't quote your pms. No outside discussion. If you have any questions about your pm or role just let me know.
