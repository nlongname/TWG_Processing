(click to show/hide)
Lynching guy seems like it would effectively be the equivalent of a no lynch, only we maybe trade knowledge about being dead for something bad happening that apparently only the wolves will know about.

Lynching Modi would presumably allow him to amp Mystic or anime tonight and we can perhaps get more info that way. If we then lynched guy, they could work together attempting to bring back people who were NK'd and eventually bring back guy himself. This is really really cool because it actually allows us to have living, confirmed humans (anime and Mystic are pretty much confirmed humans) and gives us a way to combat loss of humans via NK.

If we were to do this I don't think I would recommend reviving anyone but anime and Mystic because it's possible the wolves would NK their own members hoping to be revived. Or at least, revive those two first.

Is it possible for this plan to go wrong? Yes, and I'm almost certain it won't go perfectly. But even if we don't lynch guy at all tomorrow, we still have an active amper who has good human targets to use it on.

Killing Modi first is also important because otherwise it seems possible that guy would come back to life before he could be amped, meaning that if we wanted to use his amped power we'd have to kill him again, causing this 'bad thing' to happen twice.
TL;DR: Lynch Modi, then guy, have them work as dream team reviving NK'd humans and then guy comes back.

There's also the possibility that Modi is a wolf with a reason to want to die, but this seems unlikely.
