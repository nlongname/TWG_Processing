  • Quote
Good evening, ladies and gentlemen.

You are perhaps wondering why I gathered you all here tonight. I have read each of your publications, and understand that all of us are staunchly opposed to the supernatural or superstitious. Life is no fairy tale, and we cannot rely on more than our intellect to aid us in life. I had hoped this dinner could begin something amazing, a coalition dedicated to real, scientific progress.

It was not to be. As you can see, I have been murdered.

I, of course, know perfectly well which ones of you did it. And I have no particular mind to tell you, for what better opportunity to prove your deductive worths?

You will understand death when it comes for you.

Day 1 begins.

The rules:
WOLF GUARANTEE - This game contains real bad guys who know just how bad they are. You do not need to lynch the host, cucumbers, moral scruples, or the number 4 to secure a human victory.
VOTES - your vote is considered to be on yourself until you move it to another person. You may change your vote any number of times per day.
TIES - 2-way ties will result in a coinflip; anything greater will result in that vote being ignored and the next phase beginning.
POSTS - don't edit your posts that is cheating make a new post instead (this will only be loosely enforced, but use your judgement)
NIGHT TALKING - forbidden
PRIVATE COMMUNICATION - forbidden unless I tell you otherwise

The game:
Humans win if all of the wolves die, and wolves win if they can match the power of the human vote.
You will understand death when it comes for you.
Everyone has a role, but not all roles are equally beneficial to your team.

Sample role PM:
Quote
You are E.P.P.Hades.

You are the host. After you die, your special entitlement to just about everything means that you can communicate with all players, living or dead, both privately and publicly, as well as change the rules of the game. You also know the roles and alignment of all the players. Some might say you're overpowered, but I guess death's just like that sometimes.

Furthermore, as you may notice, there are two roles which interact with some of yours, known as roleamper and roledamper. These, respectively, strengthen or weaken some powers. I guarantee the existence of at least one of each of these roles, but not the alignment of any players with these roles.
