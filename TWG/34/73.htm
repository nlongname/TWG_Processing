Quote from: epplant on September 10, 2012, 04:01:13 PM
what is resistance?

A party game very similar to TWG/Mafia.

There are 5-10 players. Say in a 7 player game there are 5 members of the resistance (good guys) and 2 spies (bad guys who know who each other are). There are up to 5 missions that players will go on. If 3 of the missions succeed, the resistance wins; if 3 of the missions fail, the spies win. Between 2-4 players are on each mission team, and each person on the team gets to choose (secretly) whether it succeeds or fails. Even a single failure "vote" causes the whole mission to fail. So good guys will always vote to succeed. Bad guys usually vote for the mission to fail. But if they are on the mission with only 2 people on it, they will probably vote to make it succeed, or else everyone will be very suspicious of them and never vote to put them on a mission again.

How are mission teams selected? There is a leader each round who will nominate the 2-4 players needed on the mission. Then everyone secretly votes whether or not they approve.

I don't know if that was a good description, but that's the jist of it. Uniformed majority vs informed minority, but nobody actually dies (which is very nice). There are also special actions you can play (i.e. I get to see if you caused a mission to fail or succeed, I'm going to force you to vote first and publicly every single time we pick a mission team, etc).

I think it could be a decent conversion to a forum game. Maybe I will work on it and host it after eep's game.
