Google Doc with the voting records and such.

Game and Phase Rules

Players are either Resistance operatives, unaware of each other’s alignments, or Imperial spies, the informed minority. There are 6 Resistance operatives and 4 Imperial spies. The goal of the Resistance operatives is to destroy three Imperial bases. The goal of the Empire's Imperial spies is to sabotage three Resistance missions (to save their base). The game is played in up to 5 phases in which 3-5 players are nominated to be on mission teams. Everyone then votes on the nominated mission team, and the mission team supports either the Resistance or the Empire. Once either team has won three missions, they have won the game.

Nominations: Each day, the current leader (the choice is random on the first day and then passes to the next player on the list each subsequent day) has to nominate a mission team. Each mission requires a number of operatives to be on it.

The leader will make a proposal in thread. You may nominate yourself to be on the mission. Once a proposal is made, it cannot be retracted. The leader has 24 hours to propose a mission team. After a team is nominated, everyone votes on the proposed team. If no team is nominated in 24 hours, the next player on the list becomes the leader. Please format proposals in bold, as follows:

Quote
M1.1: John, Tim, Sarah
(where 1.1 is the first team nominated in phase 1, 1.2 is the second team nominated in phase 1, 1.3 is the third team, etc.)

Voting: although players are free to announce their vote or intended vote publicly, each player PMs me their vote within 24 hours of the proposal. When sending me your vote please keep it standardized for ease of use. For example, when voting YES on the second vote of day one (if the first didn’t pass), format the subject line as follows:

Quote
Vote 1.2: YES or NO

If a majority of players accept the proposal, we proceed to determine the success of the mission. If a proposal is declined, the day will continue and leadership will pass to the next player on the list. (If five votes in a row are voted down, the Empire automatically wins.) I’ll announce the results, whether we repeat the phase or go to the next, and who the new leader is. Voting is required.

Missions: The 3-5 players on the mission team will PM me their decision to either make the mission succeed (PASS) or fail (FAIL), formatted as below. Players on the team have 24 hours from the posted results of the accepted vote. Please format as follows:

Quote
Mission 3: PASS or FAIL

If a Resistance member is on the team, they must choose to support the Resistance. If sent to a spy, the spy has the option to either support or sabotage the Resistance. If there are zero acts of sabotage, then an Imperial base will be destroyed (Resistance wins the round). If there is at least one act of sabotage, then the mission was sabotaged and the base was defended (Empire wins the round). (HOWEVER, there is one base in every game that requires TWO sabotages for the Empire to succeed.).

Reveal: having received all decisions, I'll post the results. Players will not know who performed the sabotage, but will know how many acts of sabotage occurred. Once a team wins three rounds, the game ends and that team is the winner. I'll indicate the results, if the game has ended, and who is the leader as we enter the next phase, one explosion closer to the end.

Other rules:

No communication with any other player having to do with the game outside of this thread. That means that spies cannot talk to each other.
