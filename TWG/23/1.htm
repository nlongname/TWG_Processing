Y'all know that the complex is locked during the night and only someone or someones amongst yourselves could be the killer(s). You decide to kill off one person per day until you find all the assassins. You know the assassins will be killing off one person per night.

Y'all know how to play this game

Rules
1. 4 Votes per day
2. Days last ~2 days, Nights last ~1 day.
3. Phases will end at 9pm EST forum time.
4. Posting after 9 pm will be allowed until I announce the change of phase, but votes will not be taken into account.
5. Weekends will not count for day phases. If a day starts thursday, it will end on monday.
6. If a vote is tied, all players that did not vote 1 of the tied candidates will be allowed 12 hours to vote for one of the candidates. If after that time, the votes are still tied. I will kill one of the players based on random chance.
7. No night posting
8. No communication outside the game unless you are an assassin.
