The following rules are in effect:

1. No nightposting. None.
2. No PMs or outside conversation.
3. Instant Lynch: If a majority of players vote for a single person, instant lynch starts. If three players (can be voters or non voters) choose to instant lynch, then it happens. If the number of voters drops below majority, then it has to go back up to majority for instant lynch to start again.
4. Days and nights end right before midnight. Days are 2 days while nights are 1 day, if things do not happen quicker. Either way the next phase will still change at midnight. We're starting on Day 1, due to the even number of players, and it will end right before midnight on friday.
5. When you die you may make one death post exclaiming your anger at the game. Don't put actual game stuff into it.
6. Roles are secret. The theme is secret. People with roles know the theme but regular civilians do not.
7. Obviously, with how instant lynch works, you may change votes. Any time.
8. People who don't vote do not get votes against themselves. I'm sure that the nature of the game will allow other consequences for that.

Finally, this is TWG. Regardless of how much the theme makes sense with it, it's still voting during the day and bad guys killing someone at night. The bad guys can talk to each other.

The civilians are not getting PMs.
