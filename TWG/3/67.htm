"I, Garland, will knock you all down!"

You win when all 6 heroes are dead.
You lose if only you or only Chaos is alive.
The first time Chaos or you are killed, a civilian will randomly become the missing component.


"I will live forever, and you shall meet doom!!"

You are Chaos. The four fiends created you to destroy the world, and with the help of Garland, you can finally achieve your goal. he only problem is, 6 Heroes have been born with the sole intent of stopping you. You win when all 6 heroes are dead.
You lose if only you or only Garland is alive.

"The world is veiled in darkness. The wind stops, the sea is wild, and the
earth begins to rot. The people wait, their only hope, a prophecy..

'When the world is in darkness Six Warriors will come..."

You are a Hero, sent from across the ages to stop Chaos from infecting the soul of Garland, a knight. You must hide among the civilians, trying your hardest to defeat Chaos. Be forewarned, Chaos can spread in many ways...

You win if you can destroy those bent on destroying the world.
Each Hero has an item they can use to help them. Without it they are powerless!

You are the FIGHTER. Your item is the Sword. Each night you may stab someone with it. Someone will only die after the second stabbing, though, so plan carefully.
You are the Wh.MAGE. Your item is the Staff. Each night you may protect someone with its holy power, but not yourself.
You are the Bl.MAGE. Your item is the Orb. Each night you may view the secret identities of someone with its scrying magic.
You are the RedMAGE. Your item is the Tome. It contains a variety of things you can use to beat Chaos. It contains a FADE spell, which protects a hero from being killed (a civilian will be chosen instead.) It also has a LIT spell to determine the identity of someone. Finally, it has a CONF spell that shuffles the day's votes before the kill. Each spell may only be used once, but as many as you want may be cast at any given time.
You are the Bl.BELT. You don't need an item to do what you do: fight! Your superior fighting skills prevent you from being killed during the night.
You are the THIEF.  You don't have any items, and you're mad about it. Who cares if the world is in peril if you don't have any treasures to enjoy anyway? Once per night you may choose a player to try to steal from. If they have an item, you may get it. On subsequent nights you may use it.
Additionally, you are the sole winner if you survive to the end of the game and also have at least one item.
