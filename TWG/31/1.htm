TWG XXXI: The Amethyst Chessboard

  • Quote
You're an intelligent lot. Calculating. Rules conscious. Have you played chess before? Knights, pawns, and bishops, castles and even queens, all die and are removed from the game.

The King? The reigning monarch, the most important yet most limited piece on the board. The King is never killed during a game of chess. That wouldn't be civilized.

On the Amethyst Chessboard, high on a mountaintop, the White King is even more limited. He's been killed, in fact. Most lethally.

Who did it?

As the sun rises behind the Amythyst Mountain on the fortnight of the conjunction of the planets, all the beautiful onyx pieces become perfectly crystal transparent, a stunning sight. Black or red onyx and white onyx alike become prisms, shining forms transformed into globes of light. Humans come to watch the spectacle from the base of the mountain.

Today, each of you lucky tourists has a chance to control a living piece on the Amethyst Chessboard.

And who killed the White King? Why, the Black Knights, of course. With an unpredictable movement and a dark mind, a Black Knight is a wily evil villain.

Too bad they all look alike right now.

The White pieces were winning. You know there's more of them then there are of Black. You know that there are multiple copies of each piece, and that many pieces have a power (not tied to its normal movement powers). Roles are not linked to names in any way (pawns, castling, and other mysterious chess moves all make such a connection impossible).

This is expected to be a relatively vanilla game.

-Wolves (scum) are Black pieces, humans (innocents) are White pieces.
-There will be day phases of approximately 48 hours followed by night phases.
-No talking at night or after you're dead.
-No communicating with other players privately unless otherwise directed.
-Everyone should have a PM.
-No one is permitted to directly quote any PM.
-Vote as many times as you want; vote in bold.
-There must be a lynch every day.
