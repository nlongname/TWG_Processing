Being able to incriminate penut, maybe, but completely failing to provide information last night was an obvious mistake, no matter what my alignment. However, if I was a wolf, it would certainly be more convenient to, say, confirm Dieter. Instead, I took a gamble and thought I would try to view you, since I was already fairly sure on Dietcar. I forgot to extend hiding to seer-views, and that was dumb, and that mistake certainly has not been convenient.

Think about it from my point of view. If I'm a wolf, then I'm trying to spread information and confirm that me and Dieter are humans and Dr. Agon is a wolf. That doesn't make much sense, though wolves can mess up too and don't always do the right thing.

If I'm a human, then I'm trying to find new wolves and occasionally confirm humans. It's better to find a wolf than confirm a human in a lot of cases, especially if I'm already pretty sure that someone, like Dieter, is a human. Being masons with him, I had already seen that almost everything he did and said matched up with my goals and was true, though not necessarily perfect logic. So I sought to find the remaining wolves.

Humans can make mistakes too, so EITHER WAY, my actions are imperfect. So based on that alone, it looks more like a 50/50 chance from an objective standpoint, but there's also the other evidence, such as Dieter's death at the same time as Iron's, and so on.
