Let's try this puzzle.

Death Butt claims to know Mystic's color (purple) and has evidence that he knew this before last night. Death butt is Mystic's father, which is decent supporting flavor.

Quote from: death butt on January 22, 2013, 09:58:49 AM
There is nothing that says a 'spy' (I'm actually Mystic's father, not a spy, according to the flavor) can't be chocolate, though it seems to me rather pointless to tell a wolf someone's color, given they already know alignment.

My coming out as one didn't do much, but there wasn't much point in keeping it secret after Mystic died. The reason I went to the trouble of breadcrumbing it at all was so that if it did become important for me to reveal this information, I could prove that I knew it in the first day, before I could have used any night abilities.

Dieter is not claiming to be a spy. I personally don't find his analysis to be as incriminating as every else does. Actual color may not be useful (though may give hints - Mystic's potential purple could be relating to some metaphor or symbolism, like royalty, but guessing that kind of thing would be difficult), but mostly it's gonna be knowledge of color that helps.

In post 140, Dr. Agon wants to hear info from the aux roles. Could be rolefishing, but he supposedly has more info. Votes dietcar, then later says to wait for dietcar's information.

AngryNarwhal doesn't like Dietcar clearing Death Butt right away. That I can understand, though the Dietcar's reasons aren't necessarily malevolent.

Iron is a spy on Valex, according to himself. Could also be a wolf or a sorc in much the same way as Death Butt/Mystic. I understand Iron's reasoning about coming out with Valex's info, but I still think he could have waited. Would have been a gamble, but could have come out at a more useful time. Then again, I don't know when that would be.


So here's my analysis, looking at these people as best I can:

Death Butt and Iron both claim to be spies. There's no reason we can't have two spies. There's to spy/spied pairs at the moment: Death Butt/Mystic and Iron/Valex. The former seems more human (but by no means 100%) since Mystic got NK'd. Which might suggest an Iron/Valex wolf pair (which was the theory that my brain really liked, even though there's nothing really against either of them except the presence of Death Butt).

Lots of people don't like Dietcar, but I see nothing super-strong against him. AngryNarwhal seems to be operating on hunches and feelings. I don't really know what to think about the whole situation.

Death Butt's claim of purple for Mystic and Mystic's claim of not being purchasable conflict a bit. A fringe theory might be some odd breadcrumb like the purple=royalty idea, wherein you can't buy royalty. But I don't believe that.

Then there's a bunch of other people. At least one of them is probably chocolate. Peanut, anime, trientalis, and aj weren't mentioned at all in the above. I don't have a whole lot of info on any of them yet. I could maybe have a hunch, but I'd never be able to cite any evidence.

If Iron is a wolf, Valex probably is too. Though spy could be a wolf role, which would mean most of the information we have isn't all that useful. Ignoring that possibility (and, to a certain extent, including it), I actually want to go back to voting Iron though I'm probably something like 60% confident.

One more thing, and possibly further evidence for the theory:

Quote from: epplant on January 16, 2013, 01:35:24 PM
Had a fun idea about making it a bit more non-vanilla, so we'll stick it in there to see how it works. I mean disregarding the fact that this is actually a rainbow game anyways. To give you a hint, it relates to how the flavor plays out and avoiding any duplicates/made-up skittles.
