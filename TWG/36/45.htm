See, I'm not 100% convinced that it WAS such a guaranteed wolf victory under those starting parameters. I didn't realize that penut/angrynarwhal didn't feel confident about k// being a wolf, which admittedly could have changed a few things (and possibly ended things in a tie). So possibly I should have just let the game continue out to the end, but even if it hadn't been, there were a lot of possible ways for things to end. If penut had been lynched instead of AJ, it's quite likely that k// would have gone along with a "let all of my wolf partners die and then use my vote priority" plan, which would, of course, have ended in Trientalis exploding and taking him with her. Furthermore, while it was really obvious from outside of the game which players were wolves and how many there were, I think a lot of the actual M&Ms felt the risk of connecting, even with someone they THOUGHT were on their team, a lot more keenly.

If I were to redo this game, I would probably have the mechanism (wolves do not all know the identity of other wolves) and the bonding role be known publicly, which would allow humans more freedom to manipulate it. Possibly would also get rid of the provision in human hider that kills them when they hide behind a wolf, or added in something similar to the wolf hider.

It was also unfair of me to say that humans played especially poorly - mostly, penut just caused a LOT of chaos which kept people from noticing the obvious, so kudos to him for doing something i would have thought impossible.
