The gang goes to a perfectly normal restaurant for breakfast. And, like perfectly normal people they each order a perfectly normal bowl of skittles for breakfast. Everything is going wonderfully! No drama, no upsets, just everyone getting alo...

"Wait a minute," eep interjected. "Who put M&Ms in my skittles?" She stood up and looked menacingly around the room, one hand on the other, ready to remove the glove and slay the infiltrator. But no one owns up to it.

"Alright then," she growls. "I'll have to figure out which one of you did this myself. And in the meantime" - she looked down at her breakfast bowl, "I expect you to clean yourselves up before I get back."

All of you skittles shivered. How were you supposed to find the M&Ms, let alone get rid of them? You've never seen, heard, or said anything before, let alone destroyed another candy.

Nonetheless, you summon up your courage, and prepare to do what you know you must.

M&M GUARANTEE - no really i couldn't even make this up who does that sort of shit?
RULES - let's say 72 hour days and 24 48 hour nights because why not. If skittles get rid of all the M&Ms, they win; if M&Ms have a guaranteed path to eliminate the skittles, they win. change votes as many times as you like per day. In the event of a tie, the less active player will be lynched if there is a significant discrepancy (up to the host's judgment).
COLORS: optimized so that players believe they are going blind.
DEATH POSTING: Players may make 1 game-relevant post after death, but only the night after they die. All other (post-death-)posts must be game-irrelevant. Players may not post game-relevant information after their death.
PRIVATE COMMUNICATION: no player may communicate privately with another player without my express permission.
