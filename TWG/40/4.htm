Important question; what is our lose condition? Is it everyone gets oozed like you suggested in the first post? If so, that's probably going to be an important factor in our decision making.

The only control we have over how long it takes the ooze to spread is who we kill. Killing people who aren't infected hastens our demise. The next thing we want to put off is scientists becoming infected and I think that means we have to kill trientalis tonight, though again I think dr agon and mystic are also nearly equivalent options.

Tomorrow we'll have to investigate someone who isn't next to a scientist and isn't next to the person we view today. That means narwhal, dr agon, trientalis or mystic. The benefit of that is that if they come up clean, we know for sure they aren't the original ooze and if they are the original ooze they can't have infected our scientists yet so they'll come up as infected. They might also be infected by their neighbors, in which case there's a chance a scientist is also infected and the view fails, but a clean view would tentatively clear the person and their two neighbors.
