TWG XII: Murder Mansion

Creak….

Creak, creak.

Creak… pop!

Everyone jumps and the cross country tour bus slows to a stop. Outside the rain pours. The tour guide speaks in hushed whispers with the bus driver. Then the tour guide gets everyone’s attention. “It seems the bus has broken down, since its almost night time, and the rain is pouring, we won’t be able to repair it right now. Conveniently, we appear to have stopped near an abandoned mansion. We’ll sleep there for the night and then repair the bus and continue the tour in the morning.” The tour hurriedly enters the mansion, after a meal they all find an empty room and go to sleep.

Everyone wakes to the sound of a scream. Haplo, the tour guide, was found stabbed to death in the entrance. The tour bus tires and wiring have all been slashed. The rain has gotten even more ferocious. Obviously this murder was committed by a person or persons on the tour. In a fit of panicked stupidity, the group decides the only way to get rid of the murderer(s) is to vote and kill off 1 member a day, until they kill the murderer(s).


Basic Rules:
There are 2 teams, murderers and tourists.  The object for either team is to eliminate the opposing team. Rounds are divided into nights and days.  During the day, all players deliberate on who they think are murderers and cast votes to lynch someone.  During the night, murderers decide on a target and are allowed to kill someone off. The tourists win when all the murderers are dead, and the murderers win when murderers >= tourists.


Other Rules Include:
-No talking at night (This means no posting in this thread until I say the day has started)
-No private messaging to another player, meaning all discussion must happen publicly via posting. (Murderers are allowed to communicate to each other privately)
-You must post at least once per 'day', or you will automatically get a vote for yourself
-You may only vote three times per 'day'.
-The day will automatically end if one player receives a majority of votes
-Murderers and special roles must send their nighttime kills/guards/sights within a reasonable period of time, preferably less than 24 hours
-You may not copy/post any private messages from myself or other players into the game
-No posting if you are dead (this should be obvious)
-All rules will be subject to my discretion

If you do not know if you are allowed to do something, ask first!
Violaters of any of these rules will be punished!

New people feel free to ask me anything you need help with.
Green text is flavor and may possibly hold game related info.

All roles have a name associated with them. The names are meaningless.
