Flavor:
Its that time of year again. All the good children are eagerly awaiting for Christmas to come. However, Christmas might not come this year. A group of bad children have banded together in a plot to kill Santa. Its up to you good children to stop them!

Basic Rules:
There are 2 teams, good children and bad children, and a Santa Claus. The good children must try to protect Santa Claus. The bad children need to kill Santa Claus. During the day, all players will chose a player to lynch. At night, bad children will choose a player to kill. Good children win when all bad children are eliminated. Bad children win when Santa is dead.


Other Rules Include:
-No talking at night (This means no posting in this thread until I say the day has started)
-No private messaging to another player, meaning all discussion must happen publicly via posting. (Bad children are allowed to communicate to each other privately)
-You must post at least once per 'day', or you will automatically get a vote for yourself
-You may only vote three times per 'day'.
-Bad children and special roles must send their nighttime kills/guards/sights within a reasonable period of time, preferably less than 24 hours
-You may not copy/post any private messages from myself or other players into the game
-No posting if you are dead (this should be obvious)
-All rules will be subject to my discretion
