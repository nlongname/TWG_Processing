TWG XXXVIII: Dessert Planet Horror Wolves Win
« Topic Start: March 04, 2013, 07:50:51 PM »
  • Quote
Your spaceship has crash landed on an unknown desert planet. You and the rest of the colony were sent to start inhabiting a new planet, unfortunately, this is not the planet you were intended to land on. Everyone panicked after waking up from stasis. Many never even woke up. More died as you tried your best to survive the harsh conditions. However, one woman stepped up to the plant. Her name was Epp. Thanks to Epp, you folk have managed to figure out a way to live on this planet, making use of ship parts and the natural environment. You've worked out a temporary solution that should last for several, and are merely waiting for a passing spaceship to see your beacon.

Things were looking great, however all was not well. The planet's atmosphere must have affected some of your crew mates. You wake up to find that Haplo had been viciously murdered in the night. You all cry out to Epp and ask her what you should do about the murder. Perhaps, not quite right in her own head, Epp determines that you must all decide to vote on who the murderer is and then she will execute that person at sundown. She then secretly assigns 3 players to be her guards, in order to watch her back at night. And thus begins your little colony's 2nd fight for survival.

There are a few unique rules, so please read them

Rules
1. Epp is the Leader. She has 3 abilites.
     A. Epp is a confirmed Human.
     B. Epp cannot be night killed while a guard still lives.
     C. If there is a tie for a lynch when day ends, Epp will choose between one of them to kill(she can choose to kill someone she didn't vote for if she wishes).
2. After the day has ended there is no night talking. Only Epp may post in the thread, and only if there is a tied vote, in which she will choose which to lynch, there can be no trying to sway her decision after night has been called.
3. There are 3 guards to start with. When a guard dies, they will be revealed as a guard.
4. If the psychos(wolves) attempt to assassinate Epp at night while a guard lives, 1 living wolf at random will be revealed upon daybreak.
5. Each day, eveyone will vote on 1 player to lynch. At the end of the day, Epp will execute whoever has the most votes.
6. Wolves win by killing Epp, throwing the colony into chaos.
7. Humans win by killing all the wolves.
8. Unlimited votes
9. 48 Hour days and 24 Hour nights.
10. Days/nights will start/end at 10pm EST.
11. You may not post the contents of your PM(you can still make claims, but your not allowed to post the wording in order to attempt to gain an advantage.
