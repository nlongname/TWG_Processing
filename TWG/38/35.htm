Scenario 1:
Four wolves, Three guards, Epp left standing

N1:Wolves pick a guard

N3:See N1

Scenario 2:
Three wolves, three guards, Epp remaining
D1: Epp and guard lynch a wolf
N2: Wolves kill a guard
Repeat until no more wolves. Humans win.

As long as we have all our guards alive, we have no reason to attempt to out guards, as either game scenario is a win for us. I wouldn't worry about really buckling down until we lose our first guard.
