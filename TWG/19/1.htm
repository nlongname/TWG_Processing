It is late December in the cozy little village of Sunburg, Ohio. Snow has blanketed the town, just in time for the schoolchildren and students of Big Walnut Local School District to enjoy over their holiday break.

However !

Mr. Pennybottom, the village clerk, has noticed a slight discrepancy in the school district’s budget: due to a typo on the recently passed school levy, additional property taxes will be levied on the village of Sunbury, not Sunburg! This leaves the school budget with a significant budget shortfall that must be reconciled before the end of the year..

The administration (baddies) has vowed to fire one teacher (good guys) each night until the budget is brought back into reconciliation (baddies ≥ good guys). But the teachers union is fighting back!



The teachers union meets at dusk at the end of each day to vote on one employee to fire. If the teachers are able to fire all administrators, they will have saved their jobs. But, the dastardly administrators have infiltrated the teachers union, and it has become a struggle to control the fate of the galaxy...!
