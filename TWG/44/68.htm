Fuck it. You guys aren't going to change your minds about iron.

You guys lynch Iron. Game over. Endgame condition met: All Party Poopers have been exterminated.

The WINNERS: Lymric, eep, yeagz, Trientalis, xazarus


Before the reveal, a few words.
I want to apologize to the Party Poopers. I knew that they were at a disadvantage from the beginning, due to the unique mechanic I set up for them. I tried to make up for it by giving Innocent Party Goers less and less incentive to look out for the human team.
This being my first game, however, I made a number of mistakes. I severely, grossly underestimated epp/yeagz. I honestly don't even know what I was thinking. Because no single wolf was responsible for the NK, I ended up letting yeagz completely dominate them. That system also needed to be thought out a bit more, obviously.
The balance was pretty shitty. My bad. I hope you guys had fun regardless. It was fun to watch. Except for the parts where people used the logic "the game can't possibly be that badly balanced, so that can't be true." It WAS that bad. Embarrassing.

The concept of the game was to break down the entire idea of human team vs wolf team. It didn't make any sense to me, from a story telling perspective, that a vanilla would be willing to sacrifice themselves on D1 for the team. I wanted to bring the game closer to real human behavior, and that meant putting the individual over the team, in my head. Conceptually, I think the game was a magnificent success and I'm very proud.  I loved when you guys brushed on this stuff, like eep/yeagz possibly playing both sides, like the point where you threw around the idea of working with the wolves to basically choose who was alive at endgame. I think there were a few more similar things. That stuff was awesome and exactly what I was aiming for.

Anyway. The deets. Sorry for the annoying formatting, copy-paste from notepad has it's downsides.
