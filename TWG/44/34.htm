The room is in shambles. Guy and eep's battle of wits has dissolved into fisticuffs. They've knocked over the chocolate fountain and the ice sculpture of Princess Jasmine. Adderall is huddled in the corner mumbling about work in the morning. Xazarus is in tears over deathbutt's ankle. Swoop and Yeagz sit in chairs facing each other, eyes wide, unblinking. Iron watches intently. Finally, yeagz loses the staring contest.

"It's not fair, Lymric splashed vodka in my eye!" He protests. Lymric throws and orange at his head. The arguing continues.

Everyone is thirsty for revenge but everyone, especially guy, is too much of a giant sissy pants to do anything about it.

"Why don't we just kill them both?" Adderallforall suggests; guy nearly faints at the suggestion. Finally, when they've resolved the only thing to do is to have the two fight to the death using party favors, Narwhal reappears. The huge, gleaming tusk that has been surgically implanted in his shoulder sometime between now and his one thousandth post dominates the room and the attention of everyone in it. It shines like backlit silver. Eep once again starts to explain, but he silences her with a wave.

"I understanding we're having some disgreement as to which of these fine gentlemen is the beer pong champion of the week??" He croons. Eep is about to say no, the situation is much more serious than that, but he pokes her in the belly button.

"Now give me a piece of paper here and I'll see if I can work this out."

A sleek, spotless white android appears out of nowhere, notepad and spacepen in hand.
For several minutes Narwhal fusses with the paper, scratching out this, drawing large circles around that. He won't let anyone look over his shoulder. He asks yeagz for the derivitave of 2sinx^(2x), who knows it off the top of his head. THe android brings him three more drinks; two gin and tonics and a shot of fireball. He scribbles out a few things. Finally, with a triumphant grunt, he stands up. He turns the paper around.

All he has written on the paper is "6 x 9 = 42"

"Party foul dude. You gotta leave." He makes his hand into a gun shape and points at Swoop.
"Bang."
Swoop hangs his head, turns around, and lets the doors close behind him forever.

Yeagz and eep sit in the middle of the room, holding each other, crying. The rest of you disperse, wandering into other rooms and other parts of the party. The bar in this room has run dry. You needed to clear your head, anyway.

Get those night action PMs in ASAP, preferably before 24 hours have passed.

Congrats guys, you just participated in the longest D1 ever. Also we did this which is sort of neat.
