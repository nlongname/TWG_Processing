Hopefully I can clarify this without confirming anything.

In a typical TWG game, the game is over exclusively when one team has killed the entirety of the other. However, much like in football, where you can use the victory formation to run off the clock without running a legitimate play, at a point where wolf numbers equal human, it is assumed that they will lynch and NK the remaining humans that are completely powerless to stop it, assuming ties go in wolf favor, simply to spare everyone wasting time.

This is not the case in this game. The game will simply be over at that point, without assuming any moves on the wolve's part. I chose my wording in describing "endgame" conditions carefully in an attempt to make that distinction.

I do not publicly confirm that the wolf win condition is to have the same number of living players as the human team. Nor do I publicly confirm that humans alive at that point could be eligible to win, though clearly I have taken care to leave that possibility open.

Privately, everyone's PM stated their win conditions, hopefully in a clear manner, so refer to that if you have further confusion. Once again I chose the wording for win conditions carefully, so keep in mind that every requirement for win conditions has been stated in your PM. In other words, IF your PM does NOT say "All wolves must be dead at endgame for you to win" then that is not the case; I didn't just forget to mention it.
