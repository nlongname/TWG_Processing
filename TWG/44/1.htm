A long, long time ago, in a galaxy far, far away...
life was born. Since then, a lot has changed.

In fact it's been quite a long time since then. Not many people keep track of time these days, much less years. Not much reason to when any year is as good as the next. Not like you're going to die of old age, like some kind of barbarian. Time is pretty meaningless when you're immortal. 
Historians estimate it's been like, a million years since the nuclear age. Whatever. Who cares. Historians are mostly squares. I hear there's a killer party going down round Narwhal's place. It's been going for something approximating two centuries, according to him. Dat life of leisure right? The band is supposed to be killer. 

And now you're at the party. This is the craziest party that could ever be.
Don't turn on the lights 'cause I don't wanna see. The radio is blastin', someone's knockin' at the door
I'm lookin' at my girlfriend - she's passed out on the floor.

Oshit wait. She's dead. Like for real guys shit we got some shit here. Momma told me not to come.
What do we even do?!? We don't have police. This society is fucking utopian. Last time someone died is when that
dumbass Dr. Wagon went spacediving without a backup plan. What was that, eighteen hundred cycles ago? I'd almost forgotten his name.

Anyway. There's really only one course of action open to us. We have to find out who is responsible for this. We have to bring these PARTY POOPERS to justice. And we all know there's also only one way to do that.

Blood.

For the newbies' sake I will try and be thorough about these here rules.

1. I am the rules
2. Do not under any circumstances communicate with any player about the game outside of the game thread unless your PM explicitily states that you may do so. You may not "quote" PMs.
3. No death posts, no nights posts.
4. Vote by putting a name in bold. You may change your vote unlimited times until the day ends.
5. Majority rules the lynch at day's end. Ties will be broken in mysterious ways that only I understand (I promise there are rules about it, you just don't get to know.)
6. Inactive players suck and they aren't allowed.
7. Endgame occurs when either the PARTY POOPERS are all dead or the PARTY POOPERS outnumber the innocent.
8. Avatars are required. Don't change your username mid game (we're stuck with matt miller for the duration.)
9. Submit night actions no later than 24 hours after nightfall.
10. Any questions or confusion should be directed to me privately.
11. Do not edit your posts for any reason.

Once again I want to say that this game is fairly experimental, and balance is not guaranteed at all. This is also my first time hosting. I apologize ahead of time if it ends up being a shitstorm.
