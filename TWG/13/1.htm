This TWG is very different from the rest, in fact, it can barely be called TWG except for the fact that it takes the same basic idea that TWG created: a small group of the informed against a large group of the confused.

I was debating saying this based on story, but I figured I would get it out of the way right now: There are no night phases. Everything is done during the day. Both sides have actions to be doing during days ONLY. There is no night.

Almost all other gameplay rules are IN YOUR PMs. If you have any questions about your role or about game rules, please PM me. If it's something important I forgot (quite possible, there is a lot of text in this game and basic things are easy to forget) I will post clarification for everyone in the thread. Otherwise I'll just answer you.

That being said, here are a few rules that may or may not be obvious based on story, but because of our communication medium must be said:

No talking after you are dead. Obviously you don't die in real life as you do in the game, so you could still post, but please don't.

No copy and pasting of stuff from PMs. Most PMs are game related clarifications and not things from the story, and things from the story real people wouldn't be able to memorize anyway. A good way to prevent yoruself from doing this is to NOT reread your PMs right before posting. That limits you to what your character would also be limited to.

There IS a way in this game to speak to players outside of the thread. If that applies to you, please talk to that person however you wish. If it DOES NOT apply to you, you may only communicate inside of the thread.

Change your votes as frequently as you like. Voting is not necessarily as important here as normal.

This thread is LOCKED until registration finished. After that, I will unlock it and send PMs. It will still be the prologue. Day 1 will start when each player has posted in the thread saying that he has read his PM and the rules.
