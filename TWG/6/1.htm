TWG VI
« Topic Start: March 08, 2009, 07:09:22 AM »
  • Quote
Alright, alright:

A tribe of Northern Tatars is known for its prowess in battle, and the fear it strikes in the heart of its enemies by the strange attire worn in combat: wolf skin. Now, war has been declared between the Tatars and the Turks, and the demand for wolf-skin armor is at a peak. A small taskforce of elite Tatar spies have donned their wolfen furs and infilitrated a nearby wolf pack, in hopes of killing and skinning all of them. The spies are well trained in the intricacies of Wolfenheimerbunbun, the ancient language of the wolves. The wolves must sniff out and eradicate the Tatar menace before it's too late.

Basic Rules:
There are 2 teams, Tatars(wolves) and Wolves(humans).  The object for either team is to eliminate the opposing team. Rounds are divided into nights and days.  During the day, all players deliberate on who they think are Tatars and cast votes to kill.  During the night, the Tatars decide on a target and are allowed to kill someone off. The Wolves win when all the Tatars are dead, and the Humans win when Tatars >= Wolves.

Other Rules:
-No talking at night (This means no posting in this thread until I say the day has started)
-No private messaging to another player, or outside discussion, meaning all discussion must happen publicly via posting. (Tatars are allowed to communicate to each other privately)
-You must post at least once per 'day', or you will automatically get a vote for yourself
-You may change your vote twice per 'day', meaning votes are permanent and cannot be changed once cast.
-The day will automatically end if one player receives a majority of votes
-Tatars and special roles must send their nighttime kills/guards/sights within a reasonable period of time, preferably less than 24 hours
-You may not copy/post any private messages from myself or other players into the game
-No posting if you are dead (absolutely no outside discusion of the game for reasons that may or may not become obvious through course of the game)
-All rules will be subject to my discretion

Guaranteed Special Roles:
Hunter (seer) - a wolf role, allows the wolf to sniff out and identify the role of one player per night.

Wolfmother (guardian) - a wolf role, allows the wolf to prevent a Tatar attack (a successful guard will result in a message akin to "Nobody was killed last night.")
