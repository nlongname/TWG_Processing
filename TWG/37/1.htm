  • Quote
          Welcome to The Apotropaic Forest, the place which you have called home for as long as you can remember. As you awake and shake the dew from yourselves you take a glimpse around. The forest appears normal with its towering oaks and deep green pines surrounding you.  You look to the north as you do every morning, where a grassy hill towers, and wait for the arrival of the trusted Great White Stag, the protector of the forest, who makes an appearance every morning.  You all grow weary as his ghostly figure never appears. 

          One of you bravely climbs the hill and catches a horrible sight on the other side. The great protector of your forest is slumped and still in a puddle of blood.  Without a protector, what will you do? Who can you trust? Who killed him? You all look around at the creatures that you thought you knew, but did you really know them all? With the Great White Stag around you never had to worry about who was around you, but now you, the creatures of The Apotropaic Forest, need to figure out who among you are intruders.

          It’s up to you, Creatures. You must search out and eliminate the Intruders before they kill you off like your great protector. 

The rules:
-   Each of you must choose a lynch candidate once per day and you may change your vote anytime throughout the day.
-   Each day will last about 96 hours and each night will end as soon as I receive all night actions, or at the 48 hour mark.
-   Absolutely no quoting of any personal messages.
-   You may talk during the nights but not after death.
-   If you have any questions at all, feel free to send me a message.
