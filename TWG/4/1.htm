Team Fortress 2: Meet the Game

It's RED (humans) versus BLU (wolves). Defend your fortress!

During the day (rounds), RED is trying to kill the BLU team.
As is customary after the end of a round, during the night (respawns), BLU is free to kill a hapless member of the RED team.

Further rules:
1. No nightposting. None.
2. Do not post or cite PMs. Players, aside from BLU, may not PM each other or discuss otherwise outside the forum.
3. Rounds and respawns end approximately around midnight, although these may be adjusted. Rounds are two days, while respawns will be less than one day. We will begin with Round 1 once everyone has had adequate time to read their initial PMs.
4. If/when you die, you may express your disdain in a post, so long as you don't reveal anything pertinent to the game.
5. If you need clarification, I will attempt to give it without compromising anything else.

Finally, this is TWG. Regardless of how little sense the theme makes, it's still voting during the day and bad guys killing someone at night. The bad guys can talk to each other.
